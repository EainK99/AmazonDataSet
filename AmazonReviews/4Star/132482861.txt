If you are looking for a \\"moving light look\\" for a SMALL venue on a budget this light is GREAT. First of all it is LED, most smaller rooms don't have a lot of power so running a fleet of these is possible. The light is not terribly bright, but for a small room (200 people) it will look great (obviously you will need to have the house lights off and some haze in the room) I've used these lights in youth facilities at churches, most ministers want a cool look/environment for the services but don't want to spend a lot of money to pull the look off. You will basically HAVE TO USE DMX to use these lights the way they are meant to be used... DJs... This is really not a good plug and play light!
