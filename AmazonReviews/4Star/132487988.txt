This is my first wired lavalier mic and I must say that I am very happy.  I've only used it a couple times, but I was extremely happy with the results.  If you are currently using the on-board camera microphones, spend the money and get this.  You won't be sorry.  It is a night and day difference.<br /><br />One more thing, check your gain setting if it doesn't seem to be working right.  Canon camcorders ( I have the HF-G20) have their gains on auto which tends to over power everything.  Put it on manual and adjust it properly and you will get great sound.<br /><br />Here is a link to a video I made using this microphone.
