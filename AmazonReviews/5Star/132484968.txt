Others mentioned that every 4th beat is a different tone in metronome mode. To make all tones the same change \\"beat\\" to 0.  Very feature rich. This is a very feature rich metronome. It will do whatever you want: triplets, 16ths, normal mode, etc. You can increment tempo 1 unit at a time, or in the units of an old mechanical metronome. The tuner is great for guitar. Turn up the volume on the amp, or plug the guitar into the input with your amp cable and turn up volume knob on guitar so the needle will not be so jumpy.
